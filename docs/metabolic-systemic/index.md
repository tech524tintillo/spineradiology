# Metabolic / Systemic

Metabolic and systemic diseases can produce characteristic imaging findings in the spine, ranging from diffuse osteopenia and compression fractures (osteoporosis) to specific patterns of sclerosis (fluorosis, renal osteodystrophy), vertebral body deformity (Paget disease, sickle cell), and destructive arthropathy (neuropathic spine). Recognition of these patterns on imaging helps guide the differential diagnosis and direct appropriate clinical workup.
