# Post-Surgical Spine

Imaging the post-operative spine is one of the most challenging areas of spine radiology. Normal post-surgical changes can mimic pathology, and hardware creates artifacts that degrade image quality. Understanding the expected appearance after common spinal procedures, the timeline of normal healing, and the imaging features of complications is essential for accurate interpretation.
